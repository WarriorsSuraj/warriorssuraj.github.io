Welcome to My Website!

Hi, I'm Suraj, a Canadian highschooler with a keen interest in mathematics and computer science. This website is a personal project of mine where I share how I explore my passion for these fields. From detailed descriptions of projects/applications I have developed to solutions to competitive programming questions, you will be exposed to a wide variety of creative/critical ways that I have used my skills to solve problems and create amazing things!

One of the main reasons why I love math and computer science is because they allow me to creatively apply logic in order to build solutions to problems. I thoroughly enjoy the problem-solving process, and overall, the feeling of creating something useful and unique is unlike anything else.

Topics I'm interested in developing new skills in are machine learning, cybersecurity, data structures/algorithms, and calculus. Currently, I'm learning C++ and website design (through this personal project). A fun fact about me is that I love basketball, specifically the Golden State Warriors, which is where I drew inspiration for my online handle, WarriorsSuraj. Have fun exploring my website!
